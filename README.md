> [!WARNING]
> Please ask your supervisor if you are allowed to use Typst and one of these templates for your thesis or seminar paper. For this reason, it is highly recommended that you double-check the provided Microsoft Word or LaTeX templates to avoid negative effects on grading.
>
> These templates are provided on a best-effort basis. It is highly recommended that you have at least a basic understanding of Typst syntax in order to properly use these templates and resolve simple issues without assistance.

## Usage

These templates are not currently published on the Typst Universe. Therefore, they must be download and used manually:

- Download a tarball or fork and clone this repository.
- You may delete all files except the `<chair>-template` directory and the `<chair>-template.typ` file.
- Compile the document using `typst watch <chair>-template.typ` or `typst compile <chair>-template.typ` and open the resulting PDF in a document viewer of your choice.
- You may now replace all content in `<chair>-template.typ` with your thesis content.
